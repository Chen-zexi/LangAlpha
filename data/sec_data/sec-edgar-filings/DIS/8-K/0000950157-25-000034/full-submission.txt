    <div style="text-align: left; text-indent: 36pt; color: #000000; font-family: 'Times New Roman'; font-size: 10pt;">On January 6, 2025, The Walt Disney Company (&#8220;Disney&#8221; or the &#8220;Company&#8221;) issued a joint press release with FuboTV Inc. (&#8220;Fubo&#8221;) announcing
      that the Company has entered into a definitive agreement for Disney to combine the Hulu + Live TV business with Fubo (the &#8220;Transaction&#8221;). As further discussed in the press release, in addition to the Transaction, Fubo has settled all litigation with
      Disney and ESPN related to Venu Sports, the sports streaming platform planned by ESPN, FOX and Warner Bros. Discovery. Disney, Warner Bros. Discovery and FOX will make an aggregate $220 million cash settlement payment to Fubo at the signing of the
      Transaction. The full text of the press release is attached hereto as Exhibit 99.1 and is incorporated herein by reference with the exception of the section under the heading &#8220;Further Information Relating to Fubo&#8221; which is not incorporated into this
      Current Report on Form 8-K and about which the Company disclaims any responsibility.</div>

    <div><br/>
    </div>

    <div style="text-align: left; text-indent: 36pt; color: #000000; font-family: 'Times New Roman'; font-size: 10pt;">There is a risk that regulators or other third parties could bring legal actions which would prevent or delay the closing of the
      Transaction, which would trigger the termination fee described in the press release.<span style="font-size: 10pt; font-family: 'Times New Roman';">&#160; </span>Further,<span style="font-size: 10pt; font-family: 'Times New Roman';">&#160;</span>although Fubo

    <div style="text-align: left; font-family: Arial; font-size: 10pt;"><font style="font-size: 10pt; font-family: Arial; font-weight: bold;">NEW YORK</font> and <font style="font-size: 10pt; font-family: Arial; font-weight: bold;">BURBANK</font>, Calif.,
      January 6, 2025 &#8211; FuboTV Inc. (NYSE: FUBO) and The Walt Disney Company (NYSE: DIS) today announced that they have entered into a definitive agreement for Disney to combine its Hulu + Live TV business with Fubo (the &#8220;Transaction&#8221;), forming a combined
      virtual MVPD company. The Transaction will enhance consumer choice by making available a broad set of programming offerings, and is subject to regulatory approvals, Fubo shareholder approval, and the satisfaction of other customary closing
      conditions.</div>
    <div><br>
    </div>
    <div style="text-align: left; font-family: Arial; font-size: 10pt;">Under the terms of the definitive agreement, at closing, Disney will own 70% of Fubo. Fubo&#8217;s existing management team, led by Fubo Co-founder and CEO David Gandler, will operate the
      newly combined Fubo and Hulu + Live TV businesses.</div>
    <div><br>
    </div>
    <div style="text-align: left; font-family: Arial; font-size: 10pt;">
      <div style="text-align: left; font-family: Arial; font-size: 10pt;">&#8220;We are thrilled to collaborate with Disney to create a consumer-first streaming company that combines the strengths of the Fubo and Hulu + Live TV brands,&#8221; said Gandler. &#8220;This
        combination enables us to deliver on our promise to provide consumers with greater choice and flexibility. Additionally, this agreement allows us to scale effectively, strengthens Fubo&#8217;s balance sheet and positions us for positive cash flow. It&#8217;s a
        win for consumers, our shareholders, and the entire streaming industry.&#8221;</div>
    </div>
    <div><br>
    </div>
    <div style="text-align: left; font-family: Arial; font-size: 10pt;">&#8220;This combination will allow both Hulu + Live TV and Fubo to enhance and expand their virtual MVPD offerings and provide consumers with even more choice and flexibility,&#8221; said Justin
      Warbrooke, Executive Vice President and Head of Corporate Development, The Walt Disney Company. &#8220;We have confidence in the Fubo management team and their ability to grow the business, delivering high-quality offerings that serve subscribers with the
      content they want and offering great value.&#8221;</div>
    <div><br>
    </div>
    <div style="clear: both; margin-top: 10pt; margin-bottom: 10pt;" class="BRPFPageBreakArea">
      <div style="page-break-after: always;" class="BRPFPageBreak">

    <div><br>
    </div>
    <div style="text-align: left; font-family: Arial; font-size: 10pt; font-weight: bold;">Combined Business to Provide Enhanced Consumer Choice</div>
    <div><br>
    </div>
    <div style="text-align: left; font-family: Arial; font-size: 10pt;">Fubo and Hulu + Live TV each provide customers the ability to stream a broad array of live broadcast and cable networks on their connected TVs, mobile phones, tablets, and other
      internet-connected devices.</div>
    <div><br>
    </div>
    <div style="text-align: left; font-family: Arial; font-size: 10pt;">Combining the businesses of Fubo and Hulu + Live TV&#8212;which together have over 6.2 million subscribers in North America &#8212; will facilitate an enhanced choice of programming packages and
      address a variety of consumer preferences at attractive price points.</div>
    <div><br>
    </div>
    <div style="text-align: left; font-family: Arial; font-size: 10pt;">In connection with the Transaction, Disney will enter into a new carriage agreement with Fubo that will allow Fubo to create a new Sports &amp; Broadcast service, featuring Disney&#8217;s
      premier sports and broadcast networks including ABC, ESPN, ESPN2, ESPNU, SECN, ACCN, ESPNEWS, as well as ESPN+.</div>
    <div><br>
    </div>
    <div style="text-align: left; font-family: Arial; font-size: 10pt;">Fubo and Hulu + Live TV will continue to be available to consumers as separate offerings post-closing. Hulu + Live TV, a leader in entertainment programming, will continue to be
      streamed in the Hulu app and be offered as part of the attractive bundle with Hulu, Disney+ and ESPN+. Fubo, which streams more than 55,000 live sporting events annually, will continue to serve its subscribers in the Fubo app.</div>
    <div><br>
    </div>
    <div style="text-align: left; font-family: Arial; font-size: 10pt;">The combined company will negotiate carriage agreements with content providers for both Hulu + Live TV and Fubo services independently from Disney.</div>
    <div><br>
    </div>
    <div style="text-align: left; font-family: Arial; font-size: 10pt; font-weight: bold;">Combined Company will Benefit from Synergies</div>
    <div><br>
    </div>
    <div style="text-align: left; font-family: Arial; font-size: 10pt;">Following the closing of the Transaction, Fubo will be governed by a board of directors with the majority appointed by Disney, as well as independent directors. Gandler will also serve
      on the board of directors continuing as Fubo&#8217;s CEO. The Transaction will provide the combined company with the resources and support of Disney, and the existing Fubo management team will continue to focus on driving growth and profitability.</div>
    <div><br>
    </div>
    <div style="text-align: left; font-family: Arial; font-size: 10pt;">The Transaction will also enable Fubo shareholders to benefit from synergies of the combination. The combined business will realize synergies through more flexible programming
      packaging to cater to all audiences, greater innovation, and sales and marketing opportunities.</div>
    <div style="text-align: left; font-family: Arial; font-size: 10pt;"> <br>
    </div>
    <div style="text-align: left; font-family: Arial; font-size: 10pt;">The combined company is projected to be well-capitalized and cash-flow positive immediately after the closing of the Transaction.<br>

      FOX and Warner Bros. Discovery. Fubo has also settled all litigation with FOX and Warner Bros. Discovery.</div>
    <div><br>

      <div style="text-align: left; font-family: Arial; font-size: 10pt;">In connection therewith, at signing of the Transaction, Disney, FOX and Warner Bros. Discovery will make an aggregate cash payment to Fubo of $220 million.</div>

          Transaction. </div>

    Additionally, a termination fee of $130 million will be payable to Fubo under certain circumstances, including if the Transaction fails to close due to the failure to obtain requisite regulatory approvals on the terms and conditions set forth in the
    definitive agreement.
