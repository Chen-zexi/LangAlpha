(collectively with PayPal Holdings, Inc. and PayPal, Inc.&#8217;s and PayPal Holdings, Inc.&#8217;s respective subsidiaries and affiliates the &#8220;Company&#8221;) and John Kim, the Company&#8217;s Executive Vice President, Chief Product Officer, entered into a Separation Agreement (the &#8220;Agreement&#8221;). Under the Agreement, Mr.&#160;Kim will depart from the Company on March&#160;31, 2025. </p> <p style="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Pursuant to the terms of the PayPal Holdings, Inc. Executive Change in Control and Severance Plan, as amended and restated (the &#8220;Plan&#8221;), previously filed with the SEC on July&#160;25, 2024 as Exhibit 10.1 to the Company&#8217;s Current Report <span style="white-space:nowrap">on&#160;Form&#160;8-K,</span> the circumstances of Mr.&#160;Kim&#8217;s separation from the Company qualify him for a severance pay benefit, Annual Incentive Plan (&#8220;AIP&#8221;) bonus treatment, health benefits, equity award treatment and outplacement services consistent with a &#8220;Qualifying Termination&#8221; (as defined in the Executive Vice President section of Appendix&#160;A of the Plan, by reason of subsection a.). The Agreement provides for the payment of a severance pay benefit, fiscal year 2024 AIP bonus, health benefits, equity award treatment and outplacement services under and in accordance with Appendix A of the Plan, in exchange for a release of claims, continuing compliance with restrictive covenants and other valuable consideration. </p>
