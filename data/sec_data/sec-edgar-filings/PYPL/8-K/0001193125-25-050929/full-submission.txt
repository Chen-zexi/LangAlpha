strategic shift from payments platform to full-stack commerce API, PayPal has significant runway to advance the company&#146;s mission and deliver significant shareholder value. I&#146;m looking forward to working alongside Alex, Enrique, and the
entire PayPal Board of Directors.&#148; </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Drawing on her extensive career in the technology sector, Joy advises executives at global Fortune 500 companies,

serving as a member of the Board of Trustees for AnitaB.org since 2016. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:18pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B>About PayPal</B> </P>
